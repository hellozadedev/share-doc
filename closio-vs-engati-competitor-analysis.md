## Strategic Positioning Notes

### Closio Should NOT Compete On:

- Feature count (Engati wins)
- Enterprise complexity
- Omnichannel breadth

### Closio SHOULD Compete On:

- **Simplicity** - "Works in 2 minutes, not 2 weeks"
- **Meta Specialization** - "Built specifically for Facebook/Instagram ads"
- **SMB-friendly** - Self-serve, transparent pricing
- **Conversion Focus** - "We turn comments into customers, not conversations"
- **Zero Learning Curve** - No training, no flows, no IT required
